What each chart focuses on

  • Line: Trends over time (time-series).
  • Scatter: Relationship between two numeric variables (correlation).
  • Histogram: Distribution of a single numeric variable.
  • Box: Distribution and outliers for numeric data, optionally by category.
  • Heatmap: Correlation matrix between numeric variables.
